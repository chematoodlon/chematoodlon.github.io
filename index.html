 #TL;DR 

 ▮ Chematoodlon = Chemathlon +
                                                    Toodle-oo  ▮ 

We say 'toodle-oo' as a sign of goodbye, bidding farewell. And we, the chemicals, have the legacy of organising the sports event, Chemathlon.

So, presenting to you the-sporty-farewell, Chematoodlon.

Motive of this page: Presenting a basic plan for the event and inviting all suggestions & ideas by building a joint OC.

 #The Plan 

  Organising  

As the tradition goes, Chemathlon is organised by the 2nd years, and the Farewell is organised by the 3rd years. Being the amalgamation of both, this event needs to be organised by students from both the years together.

Below in the events list one can find that there are both parts, Sports & different events part as well as Farewell part including cultural events, hence, having an organising committee comprising of both years would be the best.



  Events  

We have it planned as follows:

1. Sporty part:

First Round:
Taken part by 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th years

Events:
Games like:
Arcade(PUBG Mobile, COD Mobile, Ashphalt 9: Legends, Mini Militia);
Strategy(Clash of Clans, Clash Royale);
Mini-games(8-Ball pool, Ludo King, UNO, Chess, Monopoly, Scribble);
PC games( CS Go, Siege)

Other Extra-curriculars like:
Creative Writing, Painting/Sketching, Quizzes


Qualification:
We assign points as follows:
5 - Arcade games, PC Games, Creative Writing;
4 - Strategy games, Sketching/Painting;
3 - Chess, Quizzes;
2 - Other Mini-games

Also, for more participation in each game, the final score of each participant will be decided as follows:

Final Score (out of 100) = 75 x (Points earned/Total points of all games) + 25 x (Number of games participated/Total number of games)

Based on final score we make a leader board, having two sections in 1:2 ratio:
First with only qualified 4th years, and Second with qualified from rest 3 years.

Overall, we can choose about 25 teams, each team comprising of 3 members: 1 from 1st section, and 2 from 2nd section. In this way, we ensure that each team has a 4th year.

Second Round:
Taken part by the selected 25 teams.

Events:
Fun-aptitude test about department & teachers;
Treasure Hunt; and
< Some other event if we can think. >

Qualification:
Score of each team = 4*4th year points + 2*(ΣJunior points)

We make the leaderboard leaderboard, and choose 10 teams.

3rd Round: **won't be disclosed to participants**
Taken part by the 4th years of the selected teams. Juniors can do minor help in the tasks.

Events:
Making a short 2 minutes video with the best talent you have
< We need to think of event/competition(s) where the main work will be done by 4th years, but little inputs/support given by juniors. >

Winners:
The highest vote getting video becomes the winner. Rest, the OC chooses the titles of the reamining runners-up teams.

2. Farewell part:
Basically a final get-together zoom meeting)

Events:
■ Declaration of Mr./Ms. Chematoodlon, and other titles like Best Supportive Senior, etc.
■ Sign-off speeches
■ Smriti-romonthan type event, featuring Story telling
■ Cultural part by juniors
■ Cultural part by Seniors



  Subscription  

Subscription will be solely in the form of participation fees for each event, and the whole money will be utilised for the prize-money of each event.

Participation fees will be in the form of:
 ₹5, ₹10, ₹15, ₹20 for events/games having points 2, 3, 4, 5 respectively (see the list above).

The whole money collected will be distributed out as follows:
 72% to top three (1st - 36%, 2nd - 24%, 3rd - 12%),
28% to rest 7 (7%, 6%, 5%, 4%, 3%, 2%, 1% respectively according to position).
