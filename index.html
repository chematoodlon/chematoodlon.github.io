  • Ahoy! Everyone. Chematoodlon, A new coalescence moulding two of the oldest traditions of Chemathlon, the sporting extravaganza, and Farewell of our strong and vibrant Department of Chemical Engineering, in Jadavpur University. Emerging over the years, both events have played the role of a gleamy beacon of enthusiastic exuberance and an exemplar of a magnificent atmosphere where memories are locked endearingly in the corners of our hearts where we keep moments of home.

    To vivify the same, The ship of Chematoodlon has arrived at the dock where you at the starboard will be experiencing the intriguing, picturesque and enticing sporting events arranged in a way where each can showcase their majestic gaming skills, whilst on the other side at the port we'll serve you with some captivating, eyecatching farewell ceremonies. On the auspicious 99th year of our esteemed department, we invite you to come aboard on the deck and let yourselves windward the joyous, ecstatic, and intensifying voyage of reminiscent moments, the ones which will last for a lifetime. We'll safely drop you to the other side of the excursion where you can lead,

    "from the dusk to a new dawn..."
  • HEME
  • "Yo, Ho haul together, hoist the colours high
    Heave ho, thieves and beggars, never shall we die
    Yo, Ho haul together, hoist the colours high..."    ,

    the famous sea shanty of the sailors marks the embarkment of a new journey.

    Amidst the unprecedented high waves, Chematoodlon has come out as a strapping cruise ship to pick everyone from the shore and take back on the peregrination of the estranged life in Jadavpur University one more time before they arrive at the anchor of their new careers.

    Nothing describes better the last four years than a voyage. To bring everyone along with the port of Jadavpur and find ways to the starboard, the department at the starboard when first entered. Combating the high Tides at times and scared about the destination with Teqip always playing the mentoring role of the lighthouse. The monotonicity of the ride might have caused scary sea sickness to few but overcoming it and establishing a pleasant ending on the other side is always exhilarating and what the journey was all about.
  • EMBRK ON
  • So, Here we go. Let's begin this journey!

    The ship sets sail with the 1st round of Chematoodlon. The round is open to all the undergraduate sailors. The main itineraries of this commencing part are going to be Gaming and Creative events. Discover them all inscribed in the     Itinerary page. The games range from the most popular and ferocious PlayerUnknown's BattleGrounds Mobile to the strategic Chess, and eventually down to the In-house games like Monopoly, Ludo, Scribble, etc. all of which we have played since childhood. In the creative section, showcase your writing and drawing skills, or design a meme which can crack anyone up, it's all there! Hop in for partaking in the maximum number of events as you can, as the leaderboard will comprise points from not only individual wins but also the total number of events participated. Look into the Bulletin page for more details.

    After the leaderboard is established, we will kick off with the 2nd round. The details will be disclosed once the commencing round ends. Finally, the winners will be hailed, and we will bid adieu to the beloved seniors on a virtual farewell meet.

    There's a lot on the plate to serve you all. Hence, without wasting anymore time, Register now!

    Bon voyage!
