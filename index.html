 #TL;DR 

 ▮ Chematoodlon = Chemathlon + Toodle-oo  ▮ 

We say 'toodle-oo' as a sign of goodbye, bidding farewell. And we, the chemicals, have the legacy of organising the sports event, Chemathlon.

So, presenting to you the-sporty-farewell, Chematoodlon.

 #The Plan 

  Organising  

As the tradition goes, Chemathlon is organised by the 2nd years, and the Farewell is organised by the 4th years. Being the amalgamation of both, this event needs to be organised by students from both the years together.
Below in the events list one can find that there are both parts, Sports & different events part as well as Farewell part including cultural events, hence, having an organising committee comprising of both years would be the best.

  Subscription  

Subscription will be solely in the form of participation fees for each event, and the whole money will be utilised for the prize-money of each event.

Participation fees will be in the form of:
 ₹5, ₹10, ₹15, ₹20 for events/games having points 2, 3, 4, 5 respectively (see the list above).

The whole money collected will be distributed out as follows:
 72% to top three (1st - 36%, 2nd - 24%, 3rd - 12%),
28% to rest 7 (7%, 6%, 5%, 4%, 3%, 2%, 1% respectively according to position).
